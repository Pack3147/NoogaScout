About Troop 1147

Scouts BSA Troop 1147 is a youth-led troop based in Ooltewah, Tennessee, serving young women ages 11-17. We are dedicated to empowering girls through outdoor adventure, leadership development, and service to others in a girl-led, girl-friendly environment.

Meetings and Activities

Troop 1147 meets every Wednesday at 6:30 to 8:00 PM at Ooltewah United Methodist Church (6131 Relocation Way, Ooltewah, TN). We host monthly camping trips, high-adventure activities, and regular service projects.
