Welcome to Troop 1147!

Girls Troop (Ages 11-17)

About Our Troop

Troop 1147 serves young women ages 11-17 in the greater Chattnooga are. We empower girls through outdoor adventure, leadership development, and service to others in a girl-led, safe environment.

What We Do

  • Weekly troop meetings on Monday's at 6:30 PM
  • Monthly camping and outdoor adventures
  • High-adventure trips (backpacking, kayaking, climbing)
  • Leadership training and girl-led activities
  • Merit badge opportunities
  • Service projects and Eagle Scout projects
  • Advancement through Scout → Eagle Scout ranks

Why Scouting America for Girls?

Scouts BSA provides young women with unique opportunities for personal growth, outdoor adventure, and leadership development in a supportive, girl-focused environment. Girls in our troop can earn the same ranks and merit badges as their male counterparts, including Eagle Scout!
