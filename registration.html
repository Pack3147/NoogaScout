Join NoogaScout Today!

To register for Cub Scouts with Pack 3147, please follow the steps below. There are two main components of the registration fee: the National Fees and the Pack Fees. The National Fees are required for all members and can be paid directly through the national site. The Pack Fees cover local activities and are essential for participation in our pack's events.

National Registration

To register with the National Scouts, please visit the official Scouting America registration site:

Register with Scouting America

Pack Registration

Once you've completed the national registration, or if you're renewing and only need to pay Pack Fees, please choose one of the options below:

Full Registration with Scout Life

Full Registration without Scout Life

Pack Dues Only

Important Note:

Both the National Fees and the Pack Fees must be paid to participate in Pack 3147 activities. Please ensure that all required payments are completed to fully register your Scout.
