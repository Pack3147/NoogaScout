Welcome to Troop 147!

Boys Troop (Ages 11-17)

About Our Troop

Troop 147 serves young men ages 11-17 in the Ooltewah area. We emphasize leadership development, outdoor adventure, and service to others through a Scout-led program.

What We Do

  • Weekly troop meetings on Mondays at 7:00 PM
  • Monthly camping and outdoor adventures
  • High-adventure trips (backpacking, canoeing, climbing)
  • Leadership training and youth-led activities
  • Merit badge opportunities
  • Service projects and Eagle Scout projects
  • Advancement through Scout → Eagle Scout ranks
