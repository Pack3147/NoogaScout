Join Troop 147 Today!

To register for Scouts BSA with Troop 147, please follow the steps below. There are two main components of the registration fee: the National Fees and the Troop Fees. The National Fees are required for all members and can be paid directly through the national site.

What is Scout's Life?

Scout's Life is an official magazine of Scouting America that has been a trusted resource for young Scouts for over 100 years. It provides exciting stories, educational content, and activities that inspire and entertain scouts. You can learn more by visiting their website: Scout's Life.
