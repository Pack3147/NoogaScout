About Pack 3147

Cub Scout Pack 3147 is a vibrant and active pack based in Ooltewah, Tennessee, serving the Ooltewah, Collegedale, and Apison areas. We are dedicated to providing a strong Cub Scouting program that emphasizes character development, outdoor adventure, and community involvement for all K-5 youth, both boys and girls.

Campouts and Meetings

Pack 3147 usually hosts five optional camping activities each year, providing opportunities for Scouts and their families to enjoy the great outdoors. We meet on Monday nights, 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM at the Gymnasium of Ooltewah United Methodist Church (6131 Relocation Way, Ooltewah, TN).

"Schools Out, No Pack Meetings"

We meet only when school is in session. No meetings during holidays, breaks, or inclement weather closures.
