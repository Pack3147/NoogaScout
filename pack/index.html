Welcome to Pack 3147!

Cub Scouts (Ages 5-11)

About Our Pack

Pack 3147 serves all K-5 youth, both boys and girls, in the Ooltewah, Collegedale, and Apison areas. We focus on character development, outdoor adventures, and hands-on learning experiences.

What We Do

  • Weekly meetings on Mondays
  • Camping and outdoor adventures
  • STEM activities and hands-on learning
  • Service projects and community involvement
  • Advancement through ranks (Lion → Arrow of Light)
  • Fun, age-appropriate activities for all K-5 youth

"Schools Out, No Pack Meetings"

We meet only when school is in session. No meetings during holidays, breaks, or inclement weather closures.
