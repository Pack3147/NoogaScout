About Pack 3147

Cub Scout Pack 3147 is a vibrant and active pack based in Ooltewah, Tennessee. We are dedicated to providing a strong Cub Scouting program that emphasizes character development, outdoor adventure, and community involvement.

Campouts and Meetings

Pack 3147 usually hosts five optional camping activities each year, providing opportunities for Scouts and their families to enjoy the great outdoors. We meet on Monday nights at 7:00 PM at Ooltewah United Methodist Church (6131 Relocation Way, Ooltewah, TN). Meetings are held when Hamilton County Schools are in session, so if schools are closed due to holidays or inclement weather, the pack will not meet.
